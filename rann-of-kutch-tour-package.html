Rann of Kutch

Description

Known for its Rann Utsav festival, this place has become the best place for a holiday. Originally a white sand desert, the government has built mud huts decorated with handicrafts which are the closest staying option for tourists planning to go to the festival. During this festival, travellers will get to explore handicraft stalls, food tents and attend cultural shows. One also gets the opportunity to do adventurous activities such as camel cart riding, ATV riding, paramotoring and rifle shooting.

Day 1: Bhuj Arrival - Rann of kutch(80kms/03hrs)

Arrival at Bhuj airport and later transfer to Rann of kutch, check in at Resort and rest and relax at resort in the afternoon. Evening transfer to Kutch White Rann to witness the Grandeur of Sunset. Dinner at respective Dining Area. After dinner enjoy the Culture of Kutch along with entertaining activities.

Day 2: Rann of Kutch Local Sightseeing

Today, get ready to explore the Banni and Pachcham regions and the edge of the Great Rann of Kutch. Visit Dhordo village, Bhirandiyara village, Ludiya village and Khavda village. Later visit Kalo Dungar (Black Hill) and Dattatray Temple, which provides the view of the Great Rann & sunset. Return to the Resort. Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 3: Rann of Kutch - Mandvi - Bhuj (150kms/03hrs)

Today after Breakfast we visit Day trip to Mandvi, and we enjoy Mandvi Beach, Vijay Vilas Palace, Kranti Guru Smarak and enjoy sunset at Mandvi Beach return to Bhuj. Over Night Stay at Hotel.

Day 4: Bhuj Local Sightseeing and drop at Airport or Railway station.

Today morning after breakfast visit places like Aina Mahal Museum, Pragmahal Museum, swaminarayan temple, Bhartiya Sanskriti Darsan Exhibition & Cenotaphs of Royal Family. Later drop at Airport or Railway station for your onward journey to Home.
